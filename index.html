Why you should use Public Transportation?

Public transportation is one of the highly encouraged transportation methods in the city. It is affordable transportation for everyone. It's a cost-effective option, saving you money on fuel, insurance, and vehicle maintenance. Beyond personal finance, it contributes to a healthier environment by reducing air pollution and traffic congestion. Additionally, public transit can save time, especially during rush hours, and provides opportunities for relaxation or productivity during commutes. By choosing public transportation, you not only benefit personally but also contribute to a more sustainable and livable community.

About us

Phnom Penh City Bus is committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient transportation options for all residents and visitors. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life in our community by reducing traffic congestion, improving air quality, and promoting economic growth. Our vision is to be leading provider of sustainable and accessible transportation solutions in Phnom Penh City.
