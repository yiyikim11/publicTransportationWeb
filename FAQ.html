FAQs

Use our online trip planner or mobile app to easily plan your journey. Simply input your starting point and destination, and we'll provide you with the best route, departure times, and fare options.

Passenger safety is our top priority. We have implemented several safety measures, including surveillance cameras, emergency buttons at stations and on vehicles, and trained security personnel.

Small pets in carriers are allowed on most public transportation, but there may be restrictions during peak hours. Please check our website for specific guidelines.
