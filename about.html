About us

Phnom Penh City Bus is committed to providing safe, reliable, and efficient transportation options for all residents and visitors. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life in our community by reducing traffic congestion, improving air quality, and promoting economic growth. Our vision is to be leading provider of sustainable and accessible transportation solutions in Phnom Penh City.
